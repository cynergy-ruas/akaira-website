Guests

Yogaraj Bhat

Yogaraj Bhat is an Indian Kannada film director, lyricist, producer and screenwriter. He is most predominantly known for the 2006 film Mungaru Male. The film recorded the highest box-office collections in the history of Kannada cinema and currently holds the record for the longest running film at a multiplex.

Roshni Prakash

Roshni Prakash is an Indian film actress and model. She was a finalist in Femina Miss India South in 2016. After making her film debut in 2016 with the Telugu film Saptagiri Express, she appeared in the Kannada film Kavaludaari and Tamil film Jada in 2019.

Sonal Monteiro

Sonal Monteiro is an Indian film actress. She made her acting debut in 2014 as the lead actress in the Coastal wood film Ekka Saka. She also played a modern girl role in Jai Tulunad. She featured in listings of the Mangalore most popular personalities.

Rishi

Rishi is an Indian film actor, who works mainly in Kannada cinema. An engineering graduate, he was an active theatre artist before venturing into the Kannada film industry. He rose to fame with the 2017 blockbuster film Operation Alamelamma.
